Right to Education

Why Education Matters

Education is a fundamental human right and is essential for the exercise of all other human rights. It promotes individual freedom and empowerment and yields important development benefits.

Key Points About Children's Right to Education:

  • Free primary education for all children
  • Equal access to secondary education
  • Quality education that develops each child's personality, talents and abilities
  • Protection from discrimination in education
  • Safe and supportive learning environments

Global Challenges

Despite progress in recent years, millions of children worldwide still lack access to quality education due to poverty, discrimination, armed conflict, and other barriers.

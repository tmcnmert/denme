Right to Healthcare

Healthcare Access for Every Child

Every child has the right to the highest attainable standard of health and healthcare services. This is a fundamental right recognized by international law.

Essential Healthcare Rights:

  • Access to quality medical care
  • Proper nutrition and clean water
  • Vaccinations and preventive care
  • Mental health support
  • Health education and information

Current Challenges

Many children worldwide still face barriers to accessing proper healthcare, including poverty, geographic location, and lack of infrastructure.
